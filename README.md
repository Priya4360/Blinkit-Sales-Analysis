# Blinkit-Sales-Analysis

## Dashboard: https://app.powerbi.com/groups/me/reports/3e0cd7dc-0f81-41f6-a372-b7d818de0221/175efb11d4fbef98971f?experience=power-bi

## Project Overview
This Power BI project aims to conduct a comprehensive analysis of Blinkit's sales performance, customer satisfaction, and inventory distribution using various KPIs and visualizations. The dashboard is designed to provide key insights and optimization opportunities to stakeholders, helping them improve business outcomes.

## Business Requirement
To analyze Blinkit's performance, the dashboard is focused on:
#### Sales Performance:
Track overall revenue, average sales, and the number of items sold.
#### Customer Satisfaction: 
Measure customer feedback and ratings.
#### Inventory Distribution: 
Understand how product characteristics, like fat content and item type, impact sales across various outlets.
### Key Business Objectives:
Identify trends in total sales, item performance, and customer preferences.
Provide insights into how fat content, outlet type, size, and location influence sales.
Offer a comprehensive view of all KPIs across different outlet types and regions.
## KPIs
#### 1. Total Sales: 
The overall revenue generated from all items sold.
#### 2. Average Sales: 
The average revenue per sale.
#### 3. Number of Items: 
The total count of different items sold.
#### 4. Average Rating: 
The average customer rating for items sold.
## Chart Requirements
### 1. Total Sales by Fat Content
#### Objective:
Analyze the impact of fat content on total sales.
#### Additional KPI Metrics: 
Average sales, number of items, and average rating variation by fat content.
#### Chart Type: 
Donut Chart.
### 2. Total Sales by Item Type
#### Objective: 
Identify the performance of different item types in terms of total sales.
#### Additional KPI Metrics: 
Average sales, number of items, and average rating.
#### Chart Type: 
Bar Chart.
### 3. Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales
#### Objective:
Compare total sales across different outlets segmented by fat content.
#### Additional KPI Metrics: 
Average sales, number of items, and average rating by fat content.
#### Chart Type: 
Stacked Bar Chart (for easy comparison).
### 4. Total Sales by Outlet Establishment
#### Objective: 
Evaluate how the age or type of outlet establishment influences total sales.
#### Chart Type: 
Line Chart.
### 5. Sales by Outlet Size
#### Objective: 
Analyze the correlation between outlet size and total sales.
#### Chart Type: 
Donut/Pie Chart.
### 6. Sales by Outlet Location
#### Objective: 
Assess the geographic distribution of sales across different locations.
#### Chart Type: 
Funnel Map.
### 7. All Metrics by Outlet Type
### Objective: 
Provide a comprehensive view of all key metrics (total sales, average sales, number of items, average rating) broken down by different outlet types.
#### Chart Type: 
Matrix Card.

Dashboard Overview
### The Blinkit Power BI Dashboard includes:

#### Total Sales by Fat Content: 
A donut chart showing how fat content impacts total sales, along with other KPIs (average sales, number of items, average rating).
#### Total Sales by Item Type: 
A bar chart visualizing the sales performance across different item categories.
#### Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales: 
A stacked bar chart comparing sales across outlets segmented by fat content.
#### Total Sales by Outlet Establishment: 
A line chart displaying how the age/type of establishment affects sales.
#### Sales by Outlet Size:
A pie chart showing the correlation between outlet size and sales.
#### Sales by Outlet Location:
A funnel map assessing the geographic distribution of sales.
#### All Metrics by Outlet Type: 
A matrix card offering a detailed view of all KPIs by outlet type.

### Usage
#### Once the Power BI file is loaded:

1. Interact with the filters and visuals to explore sales performance, customer ratings, and outlet metrics.

2. Customize filters for specific outlets, locations, or time ranges to derive detailed insights.

3. Generate insights that can help improve operations and strategy.
